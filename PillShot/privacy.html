Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application “PillShot” (the “Service”).

1. Information We Collect

The Service may collect the following information:

  • Medication schedules and settings: Medication schedules and settings you create or update in the app (such as your photo retention period). These are stored on your device.
  • Medication event information: Information about event status (pending/completed/skipped, etc.) and scheduled dates/times. These are also stored on your device.
  • Photos: Images you capture or select from your Photo Library. Photos are stored on your device and are not transmitted to others.
  • Information related to local notifications: The app uses your device’s local notification system to display medication reminders. The app does not provide functionality to send data to external servers for notifications.
  • Location: The Service does not collect precise location information (e.g., GPS).

2. Purposes of Use

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Displaying medication reminders: We show local notifications at the times you specify.
  • Displaying Today and History: We display screens based on medication data stored on your device.
  • Managing the photo retention period: We delete photo files based on your configured retention period.

3. Sharing with Third Parties and Service Providers

The Service does not assume that medication data or photos will be sent to external servers for use. We handle information only within the scope of iOS standard functionalities, such as storing data on your device and using local notifications.

4. Storage on Your Device

The Service may store the following information on your device:

  • Medication schedules and settings: Stored within the app’s sandbox on your device.
  • Medication event information (pending/completed/skipped, etc.): Stored within the app’s sandbox on your device.
  • Photos: Stored in the app’s storage area on your device. Photos may be deleted depending on your configured retention period and your actions.

5. Data Retention

The Service retains data according to the following policy:

  • Medication schedules and medication event information: Stored on your device. Your device data is erased when you delete a schedule or delete the app (uninstall it).
  • Photos: Photos are automatically deleted after the configured retention period has passed, starting from the time the medication was completed (or the event status changed). Photos may also be updated when you retake them. In addition, photos may be deleted when you change an event’s status (for example, returning it to “pending”). History records may remain.

6. Opt-Out

You can stop further data storage and local notification display by uninstalling the Service. You can also adjust the photo retention period in the app’s settings.

7. Security

The Service takes reasonable security measures to protect data stored on your device. For example, we store data in the app’s sandbox area to help prevent unauthorized access.

8. Children’s Privacy

The Service does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 or engage in marketing to children under 13. Because data is stored only on your device and is not sent to our servers, we cannot remotely look up or erase a specific user’s data for you. If you are a parent or guardian and have questions about this policy or how to manage data on the device (for example, uninstalling the app or deleting schedules in the app), you may contact us at the email address in Section 11 for general information and guidance. In some jurisdictions, the age at which a person can consent to the processing of personal information may be 16 or older, and parental consent may be required.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating this page. Please review this page periodically to stay informed about any updates. If you continue to use the Service, you will be deemed to have agreed to the updated policy.

10. Consent

By using the Service, you consent to the handling of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy (including any updates).

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle information, please contact us at pillshot.support@gmail.com.
